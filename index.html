About Glyphtrix

Glyphtrix is a local web app that transforms images and videos into real-time, character-based visual art in your browser.

Features

  • Image & Video Support
  • Multiple Character Sets
  • Real-time Processing
  • Customizable Output
  • Export Options

How to Use

  1. Upload an image or video
  2. Adjust the settings to customize your character art
  3. Preview and download your creation

Tip: If Density value is high, increase the Scale Factor to make the characters bigger. Pay attention to the Resolution value, higher values will take longer to render.
